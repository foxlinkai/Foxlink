01 — About
WHY
FOXLINK?

The fox symbolizes adaptive intelligence — cunning, fast, and networked. The chain link represents connectivity and composability. Together: FoxLink chains AI intelligence to on-chain action.

Built natively on Base — Coinbase's L2 — FoxLink leverages low gas fees, fast finality, and deep ecosystem integrations to make autonomous AI agents economically viable at scale.

Each agent in the FoxLink suite operates independently yet shares a unified state layer, enabling coordinated multi-agent execution across the Base DeFi ecosystem.
