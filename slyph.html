01 · Mission

What SLYPH Does

SLYPH watches the chain. It aggregates wallet behavior, protocol metrics, and social sentiment — distilling on-chain noise into structured intelligence.

On-chain data is publicly available but practically unusable. Millions of transactions per day. Thousands of contracts. Hundreds of protocols. No human can synthesize this volume into decisions.

SLYPH does. It indexes meaningful events, identifies patterns across wallets and protocols, and surfaces signals that precede market movements.

02 · Capabilities

Core Functions

Wallet Profiling
Tracks behavior clusters: smart money, retail flows, whale accumulation, exit liquidity patterns.
TVL Monitoring
Real-time TVL shifts across Base protocols. Flags abnormal in/outflows before they hit dashboards.
Sentiment Synthesis
Aggregates community signals from public sources. Detects narrative formation early.
Anomaly Detection
Flags unusual transaction patterns: governance attacks, exploit precursors, coordinated activity.
Custom Watchlists
User-defined wallets, contracts, and tokens. Configurable alert thresholds.
Report Generation
Daily, weekly, and on-demand intelligence reports. Delivered via FoxLink dashboard or webhook.
