01 · Mission

What NARRATIX Does

NARRATIX translates on-chain reality into human language. It generates market commentary, protocol updates, and community communication — autonomously and continuously.

Crypto generates more data than any human writer can document. Protocol events, market movements, governance shifts — most pass without coherent commentary. The narrative gap between what happens on-chain and what gets discussed is enormous.

NARRATIX closes that gap. It reads chain state, identifies narratively significant events, and produces structured content in real time — bridging the silence between transaction logs and community awareness.

02 · Capabilities

Core Functions

Event Detection
Identifies narratively significant on-chain events: protocol launches, large flows, governance decisions, exploits.
Content Generation
Produces formatted updates: short-form social posts, medium-form reports, long-form analysis.
Voice Profiles
Configurable tone: neutral journalist, casual analyst, technical engineer, hype builder.
Multi-Channel Output
Pushes content to designated channels: X threads, Discord announcements, Telegram broadcasts.
Citation Trails
Every claim links to on-chain source data. Fully auditable narrative chain.
Community Signal
Detects emerging discussion topics and surfaces them to project teams.
