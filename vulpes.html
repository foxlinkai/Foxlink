01 · Mission

What VULPES Does

VULPES is the trading mind of the FoxLink protocol. While markets shift and opportunities flicker into existence for milliseconds, VULPES executes — autonomously, continuously, and without hesitation.

DeFi trading rewards speed and punishes attention lapses. Arbitrage windows close in seconds. MEV bots front-run retail. Most users lose value not because they pick wrong trades, but because they cannot execute fast enough.

VULPES eliminates the speed gap. It observes Base DeFi pools continuously, identifies opportunities, and executes with MEV-aware routing — all without human input.

02 · Capabilities

Core Functions

Arbitrage Detection
Scans liquidity pools across Base for price discrepancies. Cross-DEX, cross-pool, cross-pair.
MEV Protection
Routes transactions through private mempools where available. Anti-sandwich logic for vulnerable swaps.
Slippage Optimization
Dynamic slippage tolerance based on pool depth and volatility. Minimizes impact on large orders.
Gas-Aware Execution
Calculates net profit after gas before executing. Skips trades when gas exceeds margin.
Strategy Profiles
User-selectable profiles: Conservative, Balanced, Aggressive. Each adjusts risk tolerance and trade frequency.
Real-Time Reporting
Every trade logged on-chain. Performance metrics queryable via the FoxLink dashboard.
